Insertion Sort Algorithm

1. Divide the array into a sorted and an unsorted region.
2. Iterate over the unsorted region, and for each element, move it to its correct position in the sorted region.
3. Repeat until the entire array is sorted.

Time Complexity

Best Case : O(n)
Average Case: O(n^2)
Worst Case : O(n^2)
